INTERNSHIP EXPERIENCE

My experience in teaching the students of St.Matthias School as an intern.

December 28, 2022 Divya Priya

An internship experience is a professional learning experience that offers meaningful , practical work related to a student's field of study or career interest.

During the priod of 80 days of my internship, as an intern I could understand the deep value of the teaching profession which is not possible by studying the theoretical works or studies. I could understand that every child has a different mentality and capacity. All the students are from different localities and backgrounds, and here in the school, they are organised to study in the same environment.

Diversity in the classroom was the biggest task, certain strategies were suggested by the senior teachers. The guidance form professional teachers was very helpful during the earlier days.

I could develop my teaching skills, my way of presentation of the lesson, solving problems and I learnt to have a friendly relationship with the students.

I understood that 'exposure' is very important for the student's development. It can be said that identifying a student's talents and show him/her the path for further improvements and enrichment is very important.

Lastly, I got the vivid view of what I desire to become in the future with respect to my profession.
