Feeling Belonged

Students taking initiative for the upliftment of the institution.

December 3, 2022 Divya Priya

In school, a sense of belonging gives students feelings of security, identity nd community, which, in turn, support academic, psychological and social development.

When students feel that they are a part of a school community, they are more likely to perform better academically and are more motovated to learn.
